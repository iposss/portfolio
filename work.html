Nereye

Civic Tool · Framer

Nereye is a live civic tool that helps Istanbul residents find the nearest recycling and waste disposal points by category and district.

Built entirely in Framer, the project was born from a real frustration: official municipality websites make it unnecessarily hard to find this information.

The solution — a single-page, filterable directory — went live in beta and is actively maintained.

02

Growth

Mobile App · UI/UX

Growth is a supportive habit-building app focused on flexibility and stability.

The app rejects the pressure of streaks and high-stakes perfectionism. We understand that life happens, and missing a day shouldn't derail an entire journey.

It's a mindful approach that measures progress through achievable consistency targets rather than punishing daily failures. Users are encouraged to get back on track with positive feedback.

The app reflects the fundamental cycle of human progress. It represents a continuous, supportive journey where getting back up is just as important as the initial start.

Blend

Landing Page · Concept

Blend is a landing page concept created for a modern skincare brand.

The aim was to translate the brand's minimal and elegant identity into a digital presence that feels trustworthy, calm, and easy to use.

The design process followed a structured path: from moodboard and wireframe to the final UI.
